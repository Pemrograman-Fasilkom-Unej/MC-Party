# 🎈 Party Plugin

A party system for Paper 1.21+ that integrates seamlessly with RuneMobs, Jobs, and provides custom chat prefixes. Easily group up with friends, share experiences, and communicate privately!

## ✨ Features

- **Party Creation & Management**: Create parties, invite players, and manage your group easily.
- **Private Party Chat**: Chat exclusively with your party members using the `/partychat` command.
- **Cross-Plugin Integration**: Designed to work alongside RuneMobs and Jobs.
- **Custom Chat Prefixes**: Distinctive chat tags to easily identify party members in global chat.

## 🎮 Commands

- `/party` (Alias: `/p`): Main command for party management.
  - `/party create`: Create a new party.
  - `/party invite <player>`: Invite a player to your party.
  - `/party join <player>`: Join an open party or accept an invitation.
  - `/party leave`: Leave your current party.
  - `/party list`: View current members of your party.
- `/partychat <message>` (Alias: `/pc`): Send a message exclusively to your party.

## 🛠 Installation

1. Drop the compiled `Party.jar` into your server's `plugins/` directory.
2. Restart or reload your server.
3. Group up and enjoy!
